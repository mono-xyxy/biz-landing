THE PROBLEM

AI tools are
everywhere.
Value is not.

Every week a new model drops. Every month a new platform claims to transform your workflow. And yet, most teams still struggle to get real, reliable AI working inside the things they actually build.

The tools are either too raw (bare APIs with no guardrails) or too opinionated (locked-in platforms that work great in demos and fail in prod).

We think there's a missing layer. Not another wrapper. Not another chatbot UI. Something fundamental — that makes AI-powered software feel as reliable as non-AI software. Observable, testable, composable.

"The goal isn't to ship another AI product. It's to build the thing that makes the next hundred products possible." — Founding team note, January 2025
WHAT WE BELIEVE
  • 01

    AI should be boring infrastructure

    Like databases or message queues — reliable, predictable, quietly doing its job. Not a magic trick.

  • 02

    Observability first, always

    If you can't see exactly what your AI is doing and why, you can't trust it in production. Full stop.

  • 03

    Small teams deserve enterprise power

    The tools Fortune 500 teams build with should be accessible to a 3-person startup in Bangalore.

  • 04

    Models are commodities. Orchestration is the moat.

    GPT-5 or Claude 4 — it won't matter. What matters is how you chain, route, and validate them.
